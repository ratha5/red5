OUR PRODUCTS

Inspiring the World to Cook More


Saladmaster offers a cooking system that has inspired generations of culinary enthusiasts. Whether you’re new to the kitchen – or a seasoned expert – you’ll appreciate products engineered for superior performance and strength.



  • Our advanced semi-vacuum system cuts cooking time in half.
  • Vapo-ValveTM technology retains up to 93% of food's essential vitamins and minerals.
  • The thermal core center exceeds standards for even heating performance.
  • 316Ti stainless steel combines 316 stainless steel with titanium for an interior surface that protects the natural textures, flavors and nutrients of food.
  • Saladmaster cookware comes with a Limited Lifetime Warranty.
